Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our DJ services

How far in advance should I book?

3-6 months for weddings, 1-2 months for other events. We can often accommodate last-minute bookings.

Do you take music requests?

Absolutely! We create custom playlists and have QR code systems for guest requests.

Do you travel outside Hampshire?

Yes, we serve Hampshire and surrounding areas. Travel fees may apply for distant locations.

What's your cancellation policy?

30+ days: Full refund • 14-30 days: 50% refund • 7-14 days: 25% refund

Do you provide equipment setup?

Yes, we handle all setup and teardown. We arrive early to ensure everything is perfect.

Are you fully insured?

Yes, we carry full public liability insurance for complete peace of mind.
