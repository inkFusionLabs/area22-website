About Area22

Your trusted partner for unforgettable events

Our Story

Area22 was born from a passion for music and a desire to create unforgettable experiences. Founded in the heart of Basingstoke, we've been bringing the perfect mix of music and atmosphere to events across the region for over a decade.

What started as a small operation has grown into one of the most trusted names in professional DJ services, known for our attention to detail, extensive music library, and commitment to making every event special.

We believe that great music has the power to transform any gathering into an extraordinary celebration, and we're dedicated to ensuring that every client receives the personalized service they deserve.
